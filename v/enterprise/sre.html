SITE RELIABILITY ENGINEERING

THE SRE APPROACH IS APPROPRIATE TO ALL CLOUD USERS.

Site Reliability Engineering improves the reliability of currently running software, while minimizing the work involved maintaining it.

Automating as many tasks as possible allows operations experts to provide strategic, higher-level work, such as planning a new deployment or creating a pipeline for faster product feedback.

Some of the typical features of adapting site reliability engineering:

  • Fill the gap between developer and sysadmin.
  • Automate the process.
  • Continuously monitor and analyze application performance.
  • Plan and maintain operational runbooks.
  • Contribute to overall product roadmap.
  • Ensure software has good logging and diagnostics.
