Critical data inputs for overall capital and operations are necessary in the planning and preparation of expenditure that cover infrastructure.

OSS provides data such as network availability and network performance information, allowing operators to manage networks and systems to deliver positive user experiences on any connection.

The BSS provides the capability for dealing with customers, supporting processes such as taking orders, processing bills, and collecting payments, subscription information such as new sign-ups, churn and user demographics as well as information relating to customer relationship management.
